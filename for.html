introduction of formosan black bears


Appearance

Their biggest feature is the white V-shaped imprint on their chests.

They have five long feet and long sharp claws, which can easily outrun humans.

Distribution and Habitat

Taiwan black bears are forest animals with a wide range of activities, mainly distributed in the central mountain range.

Behavior

They have a good sense of smell, are mobile, and build dens in trees.

They don't hibernate because they have food all year round.

Diet

They are omnivore animals but plant food is their main food.

Breeding

They are solitary animals.Except for the breeding season, Formosan black bears do not have a fixed den.

Threat

They are now threatened by habitat loss, illegal hunting and a lake of funding.

All of the above reasons will reduce the number of formosan black bears.
