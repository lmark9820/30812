introduction of penghu ancient elephants


What kind of elephant is it ?

It's an ancient elephant, and the molar plates of the elephant were grouned into diamond-shape.

How is it different from the mammoth ?

We can tell from their tusks, mammoth's tusks are twisted and curved.

However, this kind of elephant's are straight and curved.

Where and whom were its fossil bones discovered ?

They were found in the Penghu channel, which is between Penghu and Taiwan.

The fisherman found it about 40 years ago, and they also found deer and buffalo.

Why did their fossil bones end up in the ocean ?

Originally, these animals live in northern China, and they migrate to the south.

However the sea level came back, so they ended up in the ocean.

What age did they live in ?

They lived in the ice age 15000 years ago, the land bridge was born, most of the water was frozen, it was too cold, so they midgrated to the south.

Their diet and fun fact

They eat roots, grass, bark, and fruits.

The oldest elephant will lead the herd and related females live with their young in the herds.
