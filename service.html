What we have learn

English skills

With the full English teaching method, we not only increased our knowledge of the exhibitions, but also learned lots of English words and phrases in biology, architecture, culture, etc.

Teamwork

Unity is strength... when there is teamwork and collaboration, wonderful things can be achieved. By working in groups for two semesters, we make ourselves stronger by cooperating with each other.

Exhibitions

Most people only rushed through when they visited the museum, but through thus course, the teacher took us to have a deep understanding of the various exhibits in the museum.

Final presentation

At the end of the semester, we improve our oral English ability by randomly drawing questions and giving reports on stage, which made us more familiar with the exhibition we have learned.
