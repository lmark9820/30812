introduction of giant squids


Size and why is the exhibit there

It's about 9 meters long and about 240 kilograms in weight.

It was captured at the coast in New Zealand and was donated to the museum.

Natural enemy

Giant squid's natural enemy is whales.

Because scientists have found some remnants in the whale's stomach : beaks, which is hard to digest.

Features

Squid has a triangular-shaped head,and has eight arms and two tentacles, which allow it to hunt .

They also have huge and developed eye and suction rings, which are weapons to grab the prey.

Why we know less about it ?

Because they live in the deep ocean about 500 - 1000m .

And they appear from DEC. to MAR. of the coast of New Zealand to reproduce.But from APR. they disappear.

Defense

Squid will squeeze out ink to defend themselves when they are in danger.

Similar animals

Octopus : They have eight legs, can spray black ink and have many suckers on the arms and legs, no inner shell.
Cuttlefish : They're inner shell is calcareous , white and their stabilizing fins aren't connected.
Neritic Squid : Their inner shell is chitin , transparent.
