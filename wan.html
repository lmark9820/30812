introduction of wan fu kon


Meaning of the name and worship gods

The name, Wan Fu Kon means ten thousand, fortune, and it's a lucky name.

People worship five patrol gods there and it protects people from illness and misfortune.

The animal with a dragon head and a fish body

It's the nine offspring of dragon and the hybrid of fish and dragon.

It can swallow all evil influences like fire.

The caisson ceiling (spider web ceiling)

An architectural feature typically found in the ceiling of temples.

It's often layed and richly decorated.

Lotus in the center of the cassion ceiling

There is a hole in the center of a lotus.

It's to let the smoke go out and people's prayers can go to heaven through it.

Carving on the altar

Peony: It's a symbol of wealth, power and the vase is pronounced the same as peace.

Rats and pumpkins: Mean fertility because there are a lot of seeds inside pumpkins, and rats can give birth to a lot of babies.

Red moon blocks

Red moon blocks can express god's answer to worshiper's question.

People will toss a pair of red moon blocks onto the floor. And they are typically tossed about one to three times for any particular question.
